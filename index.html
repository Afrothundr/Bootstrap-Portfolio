About Me

I’m a web developer, producer, and content curator.

I’m also an imaginative perfectionist who makes up words to songs I already know.

The future of online content is smaller, more personable—story driven. I want to be there to craft the next big something you talk about at your cousin’s BBQ or show to strangers in line at the DMV. For me, the best user experiences are rooted in research, the best campaigns have attainable goals, and the best ice cream flavor is mint chocolate chip black walnut.
